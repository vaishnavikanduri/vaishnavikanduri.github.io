// 00 — About

About me.

I completed my MSc in Artificial Intelligence at the University of Southampton, where the curriculum took the field seriously from both directions — the mathematics underneath modern AI and the engineering discipline that turns theory into systems that actually run. Coursework in Deep Learning, Computer Vision, and Reinforcement Learning sat alongside heavier work in optimisation and probabilistic reasoning.

My technical home is Python with PyTorch — training models, debugging gradients, and building the data pipelines that quietly determine whether anything else works. My dissertation on automatic aneurysm segmentation using convolutional neural networks pushed me deep into medical imaging — handling volumetric scans, navigating severe class imbalance, and learning that the gap between a model that scores well on a benchmark and one a clinician could plausibly trust is large, and worth respecting.

I'm now looking for an entry-level role as a Machine Learning Engineer, Data Scientist, or AI Developer — somewhere I can contribute from day one and grow alongside people who care about doing the work well.
